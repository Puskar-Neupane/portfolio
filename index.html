Puskar Neupane

Electrical Engineer, Researcher

Hello, I am an Electrical Engineer with a deep passion for making power systems more resilient and sustainable. My mission is to mitigate the challenges that arise during the integration of renewable energy sources into the grid, particularly in power systems with high renewable penetration and low inertia. I am dedicated to using my skills to solve real-world problems and develop solutions that positively impact our society, country, and the world. Let's connect to create a better future for all..
