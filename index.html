Projects

  • Virtual Oscillator Control for Inverters (Apr. 2023 – Mar. 2024)
    Designed a VOC control system for a three-phase inverter and operated multiple of them in parallel. Analyzed the behaviors of a VOC (Grid-Forming) and PV (Grid-Following) connected microgrid. Compared the performance of the VOC-integrated microgrid against conventional droop control.
  • Forecasting Losses in Magnetic Materials (Apr. 2023 – Dec. 2024)
    Completed as part of IEEE PELS-Google-Enphase-Princeton MagNet Challenge 2023. Prediction of loss based on magnetic field, temperature, and frequency of operation. Used LSTM to train the model in the frequency domain.
